Pergande Lab

Chemical biology and mass spectrometry-based target discovery at Boston University.

Research Overview

The Pergande Lab studies molecular mechanisms driving neurodegenerative disease, with a focus on glial cells, protein aggregation, and mass spectrometry-based multiomics.

Research Areas

Neurodegeneration

Modeling disease-relevant mutations and protein aggregation.

Glial Biology

Studying astrocytes, oligodendrocytes, and microglia.

Multiomics

Proteomics, lipidomics, and metabolomics.

Target Discovery

Chemical biology and mass spectrometry approaches.
